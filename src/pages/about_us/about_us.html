About Us About the OpV
Comcast Operational Vitals operations has been taken up for measuring and reporting health of various parameters through Revenue Assurance flow for HSD (High Speed Data) residential customers. This app is intended to provide details around various KPIs and Trends being measured as part of Operational Vitals operations.

Comcast Corporation is a U.S.-based multinational mass media company and largest broadcasting and Cable Company in the world by revenue. It is the largest cable company and home Internet service provider in the United States and the nation's third largest home telephone service provider.

Comcast intends to move away from unlimited data plans to Usage Based Billing for High Speed Internet (HSI) service for residential customers. New data plans would have data allowance of 5GB and 300GB in most trial markets and could go up to 600GB in selected markets.

Comcast expects annual revenue of $4 Billion from UBB business.
